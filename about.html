About Kenya Safari Tours

Discover the story behind Kenya's premier safari experience

Meet Cavin Otieno

A passionate safari guide and travel expert with over 10 years of experience in showcasing the magnificent beauty of Kenya to travelers from around the world.

Born and raised in Kenya, Cavin grew up with the rich wildlife and diverse landscapes of his homeland. His journey from a local wildlife enthusiast to a professional tour operator has been driven by a deep love for Kenya's natural heritage and a desire to share its wonders with others.

With extensive knowledge of Kenya's national parks, reserves, and hidden gems, Cavin has guided thousands of visitors through unforgettable safari experiences. His expertise spans from the iconic Masai Mara to the pristine beaches of Lamu Island, ensuring every traveler discovers the magic of Kenya.
